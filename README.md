Privacy Policy


- Tajani Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates the mobile application Accessories Record Manager (the "Application"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application and the choices you have associated with that data.

- By downloading and using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. 100% Offline Local Storage & Data Privacy
The Application is designed as an offline-first local record utility.

Your Data Stays on Your Device: All business transactions, buying prices, selling prices, inventory logs, and profit calculations are stored solely on your local device's internal storage using a secure local database.

No Cloud Uploads: None of your entered business records, inventory data, or financial histories are uploaded, transmitted, or hosted on external web servers, databases, or cloud environments.

We have no access to your business records.
No User Accounts: You can use the Application without registering an account, providing an email, or entering any personal identification details.

3. Information Collection and Use
While the core features of the Application work completely offline and do not collect any personal data, the Application integrates third-party software development kits (SDKs) that may collect data to facilitate system performance and display advertisements.

A. Third-Party Service Providers
We may employ third-party companies and APIs. These third-parties may have access to certain device identifiers and telemetry data solely to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
Google Play Services: Used to facilitate app delivery, updates, and core Android system integration.

Google AdMob: We utilize Google AdMob to display advertisements in the Application to support ongoing development and maintenance. Google AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), cookies, and limited network telemetry data to display personalized or non-personalized ads.
For more information, please review the respective privacy policies:
Google Play Services Privacy Policy
Google AdMob Privacy Policy

5. Permissions Requested
To function correctly, the Application may request the following system permissions on your device:
Internet access (android.permission.INTERNET): Used strictly by the Google AdMob SDK to fetch and render advertisements. The application does not use internet permissions to upload your records.
Network State (android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): Used to check if your device is connected to the internet to load ads correctly.

7. Children’s Privacy
The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any user database or account information, we store zero personal information on young audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided any telemetry data, please contact the third-party providers (like Google AdMob) or reach out to us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
